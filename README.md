Online Shopping Data Analysis Project

Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing an online shopping dataset to extract meaningful business insights related to customer behavior, sales trends, and revenue performance.
The analysis is divided into to demonstrate a complete data analytics workflow.

Objectives

Understand customer distribution across cities and states
Analyze sales and order trends over time
Evaluate revenue contribution by product categories
Study customer purchasing behavior and retention
Generate strategic insights for business decision-making

Dataset Description

Customers (city, state)
Orders (date, order ID)
Products (category, price)
Payments (installments)
Sellers (revenue)


Key Insights

Sales show strong seasonal trends across months and years
A few product categories contribute to a major share of revenue
High-value customers significantly impact overall sales
Customer retention is moderate, indicating scope for improvement
Certain sellers dominate revenue generation

Business Impact

Helps improve inventory planning and demand forecasting
Identifies high-value customers and top-performing sellers
Supports data-driven marketing strategies
Reduces stockout risks and improves customer satisfaction

Conclusion

This project demonstrates end-to-end data analysis, from basic exploration to advanced business insights. 
It highlights how data can be used to optimize operations and improve decision-making in the e-commerce domain.
